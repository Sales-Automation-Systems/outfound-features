# Campaign Intelligence

### Unified Visibility Across Every Sending Platform

Outfound doesn't send emails -- your sending platforms do. What Outfound does is ingest all campaign data from Instantly, Smartlead, and EmailBison and give you a single, unified view of everything happening across your outbound operation. Every sequence, every variant, every schedule, every account -- visible in one place regardless of where the emails are actually sent.

---

## Multi-Step Sequence Tracking

Outfound ingests and tracks multi-step email sequences from your sending platforms, including configurable delays between steps. You see how many touchpoints each prospect receives and where in the sequence they engage.

**Why it matters:** Most deals don't happen on the first email. Outfound tracks every step of every sequence across every platform, so you know exactly where in your cadence prospects engage -- without logging into three different tools to piece it together.

---

## A/B Variant Tracking

Each step in a sequence can have multiple variants. Outfound tracks each variant separately across all metrics -- sent, opened, replied, bounced, and even attributed conversions.

**Why it's a game-changer:** Your sending platform tells you which variant "won." Outfound tells you which variant generated actual meetings and revenue. Combined with [Copy & Offer Analytics](../analytics/copy-offer-analytics.md), variants are automatically tagged by AI across 7+ copy dimensions, so you don't just know *which* variant won -- you know *why*.

---

## Schedule & Throttle Visibility

Outfound captures campaign scheduling configurations from your sending platforms:

- **Daily send limits** -- How many emails are capped per day per campaign
- **Daily max leads** -- How many new prospects enter the sequence each day
- **Email gap timing** -- Minimum time between emails to the same prospect
- **Send windows** -- When emails are going out

**Why it matters:** Deliverability is everything in cold email. Outfound gives you visibility into the scheduling and throttling settings across all your campaigns and platforms from one dashboard -- so you can spot misconfigurations before they damage your domains.

---

## Reply Intelligence

Outfound detects and classifies every reply that comes in from your sending platforms. It distinguishes between human replies, auto-replies (out-of-office, delivery failures), and bot responses -- then routes them to the appropriate workflow: [Slack notifications](../integrations/slack.md), [Auto-Responders](../automation/auto-responders.md), or [CRM sync](../integrations/crm-hubspot.md).

**Why it matters:** A reply isn't just a reply. Outfound classifies the *quality* of every response so your team can prioritize what matters. A positive human reply and an out-of-office auto-reply are completely different signals, and Outfound treats them accordingly.

---

## LinkedIn Campaign Tracking

Outfound extends beyond email to LinkedIn outreach tracking:

- LinkedIn account management
- Prospect tracking specific to LinkedIn activity
- Conversation management
- Dedicated analytics

**Why it matters:** Modern outbound is multi-channel. Tracking email and LinkedIn from the same platform means unified prospect tracking, no duplicate outreach analysis, and a single view of every touchpoint across channels.

---

## Multi-Platform Unification

This is the core value of Outfound's campaign layer. It integrates natively with:

- **Instantly.ai**
- **Smartlead.ai**
- **EmailBison**

All campaign data from every platform flows into Outfound's unified data model. You see the same analytics, the same attribution, and the same lead database regardless of which tool actually sent the email.

**Why it's a game-changer:** Most teams outgrow one sending platform or use multiple for different clients. Outfound is the single source of truth across all of them. Switch platforms, run multiple simultaneously, or migrate between them -- your data stays unified and your reporting stays consistent. The sending platform becomes interchangeable; your intelligence layer stays permanent.

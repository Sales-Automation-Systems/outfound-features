# Campaigns

### Complete Multi-Channel Campaign Management

Outfound gives you full control over your outbound campaigns -- email sequences, LinkedIn outreach, A/B variant testing, sending schedules, and per-account throttling -- all managed from a single interface that works across multiple sending platforms.

---

## Multi-Step Email Sequences

Every campaign in Outfound supports multi-step email sequences with configurable delays between steps. You define how many touchpoints a prospect receives and how many days between each one.

**Why it matters:** Most deals don't happen on the first email. Sequences let you build follow-up cadences that feel natural, not robotic. Outfound handles the timing, the tracking, and the analysis of every step independently -- so you know exactly where in your sequence prospects engage.

---

## A/B Variant Testing

Each step in a sequence can have multiple variants. Outfound tracks each variant separately across all metrics -- sent, opened, replied, bounced, and even attributed conversions.

**Why it's a game-changer:** Other tools let you A/B test subject lines. Outfound lets you A/B test anything -- the full email body, the CTA, the value proposition, the tone -- and then correlates each variant with actual business outcomes (meetings booked, deals closed). This is real copy optimization, not vanity metric testing.

Combined with Outfound's [Copy & Offer Analytics](../analytics/copy-offer-analytics.md), variants are automatically tagged by AI across 7+ copy dimensions, so you don't just know *which* variant won -- you know *why*.

---

## Smart Scheduling

Campaigns come with detailed scheduling controls:

- **Daily send limits** -- Cap the total emails sent per day per campaign
- **Daily max leads** -- Control how many new prospects enter the sequence each day
- **Email gap timing** -- Set minimum time between emails to the same prospect
- **Custom send windows** -- Define when emails go out (business hours, time zones)

**Why it matters:** Deliverability is everything in cold email. Sending too fast, at the wrong times, or to too many prospects at once gets you flagged. Outfound's scheduling engine ensures your campaigns respect the limits that keep your domains healthy and your emails landing in inboxes.

---

## Stop-on-Reply Intelligence

Campaigns automatically stop the sequence when a prospect replies. Outfound also detects auto-replies (out-of-office, delivery failures) and can handle them differently -- pausing instead of stopping, or routing them to your [Auto-Responder](../automation/auto-responders.md) system.

**Why it matters:** Nothing kills trust faster than following up after someone already responded. Outfound's reply detection is precise, distinguishing between human replies, auto-replies, and bot responses, so you never send an awkward follow-up to someone who already said yes.

---

## LinkedIn Campaigns

Outfound extends beyond email to LinkedIn outreach. LinkedIn campaigns have their own management layer with:

- LinkedIn account management
- Prospect tracking specific to LinkedIn activity
- Conversation management
- Dedicated analytics

**Why it matters:** Modern outbound is multi-channel. Running email and LinkedIn from the same platform means unified prospect tracking, no duplicate outreach, and a single view of every touchpoint across channels.

---

## Multi-Platform Operation

Outfound isn't locked to one sending tool. It integrates natively with:

- **Instantly.ai**
- **Smartlead.ai**
- **EmailBison**

All campaign data from every platform flows into Outfound's unified data model. You see the same analytics, the same attribution, and the same lead database regardless of which tool actually sent the email.

**Why it's a game-changer:** Most teams outgrow one sending platform or use multiple for different clients. Outfound is the single source of truth across all of them. Switch platforms, run multiple simultaneously, or migrate between them -- your data stays unified and your reporting stays consistent.

---

## Text-Only Mode

For campaigns where HTML formatting hurts deliverability, Outfound supports text-only email mode. No tracking pixels, no HTML -- just plain text that looks like a real human sent it.

**Why it matters:** Some audiences and email providers are suspicious of HTML-heavy cold emails. Text-only mode maximizes inbox placement for high-value, personalized outreach where deliverability matters more than open tracking.

---

## Per-Account Sending Controls

Every email account connected to a campaign has its own sending limits and capacity tracking. Outfound monitors how many emails each account sends daily and ensures no single account is overloaded.

**Why it matters:** Email account health is fragile. One overloaded account can damage an entire domain's reputation. Outfound's per-account controls distribute the load intelligently, keeping every account within safe sending thresholds.

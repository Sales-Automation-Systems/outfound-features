## Five Dimensions of Analytics

### 1. [Campaign Analytics](campaign-analytics.md)
Performance metrics across every campaign, every variant, every step. Sent, replied, bounced, opened, clicked -- broken down by time period, client, campaign, and variant. Rolling windows (1-day, 3-day, 7-day, 30-day) for trend analysis. Baseline percentiles so you know if a campaign is performing above or below your historical norms.

### 2. [Infrastructure Analytics](infrastructure-analytics.md)
Domain health, mailbox performance, bounce rates, disconnection trends, sending capacity utilization. Know which email accounts are healthy, which ones are struggling, and which domains need attention -- before deliverability problems show up in your reply rates.

### 3. [Copy & Offer Analytics](copy-offer-analytics.md)
AI-powered structural analysis of your email copy. Every sequence is automatically tagged across 7+ dimensions -- subject line style, opening approach, value proposition, social proof, CTA type, tone, and structure. Then those tags are correlated with performance metrics. This isn't A/B testing one variable. This is understanding which combination of copy elements drives the best results.

### 4. [Lead Category Analytics](lead-category-analytics.md)
AI-classified reply categorization that goes beyond "replied" or "didn't reply." Every response is classified as human or bot, positive or negative or neutral, and tagged with a specific category. You see the quality of your replies, not just the quantity.

### 5. [Mailbox Health Scoring](mailbox-health.md)
Per-mailbox health scores with percentile benchmarking against your own portfolio. Reply rates, bounce rates, sentiment breakdowns, and disconnection tracking -- for every single email account. Trend analysis across multiple time windows shows whether accounts are improving or declining.

## Real-Time OLAP Engine

Behind Outfound's analytics is a dedicated OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) data warehouse. This isn't a SQL database running slow aggregation queries -- it's a columnar analytics engine purpose-built for fast, complex queries over large datasets.

**What this means for you:**
- Analytics refresh every 3 minutes -- you're never looking at stale data
- Queries over millions of records return in milliseconds
- Pre-aggregated metrics tables eliminate the wait time for common views
- Rolling time windows (daily, weekly, monthly) are always ready

**Why it's a game-changer:** Most outbound tools run analytics against their production database, which means slow dashboards and limited historical depth. Outfound's dedicated analytics engine means you can drill into years of data across any dimension without waiting.

## Connected Analytics

The real power of Outfound's analytics isn't any single dimension -- it's that they're all connected:

- See a campaign with a great reply rate → drill into **copy analytics** to understand which elements drove it
- Notice a spike in bounces → check **infrastructure analytics** to find the problematic accounts
- See attribution events → trace them back to **campaign variants** that generated them
- Identify a high-performing lead category → understand the **copy patterns** that trigger it

Other tools give you siloed metrics. Outfound gives you a complete analytical picture of your outbound operation.

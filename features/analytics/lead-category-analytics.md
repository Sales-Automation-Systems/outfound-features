# Lead Category Analytics

### AI-Powered Reply Classification That Goes Beyond "Replied"

Most outbound tools tell you someone replied. Outfound tells you *how* they replied -- was it a human or a bot? Was it positive, negative, or neutral? Was it a meeting request or an out-of-office? Lead category analytics gives you the quality of your responses, not just the quantity.

---

## How Lead Categorization Works

Every reply that comes into Outfound is automatically classified along three axes:

### 1. Response Type
- **Human** -- A real person wrote this response
- **Bot** -- An automated system sent this (auto-reply, out-of-office, delivery notification)

### 2. Sentiment
- **Positive** -- Interest, willingness to engage, meeting request
- **Negative** -- Not interested, unsubscribe request, complaint
- **Neutral** -- Question, information request, ambiguous response

### 3. Category
Specific categories like:
- Interested / wants to learn more
- Meeting booked
- Not interested
- Out of office
- Wrong person / wrong company
- Auto-reply / delivery failure
- Already a customer
- Referred to someone else

---

## What the Analytics Show

### Category Distribution
See the breakdown of all replies across categories. Instantly understand what percentage of your responses are genuinely positive vs. auto-replies vs. not-interested.

**Why it matters:** A "20% reply rate" means nothing if 15% of those replies are out-of-office messages. Lead category analytics reveals your *real* engagement rate -- the percentage of replies that actually represent pipeline opportunities.

### Sentiment Analysis
View aggregated sentiment across campaigns, clients, and time periods:
- **Human positive** -- Real interest from real people
- **Human negative** -- Genuine rejections
- **Human neutral** -- Ambiguous or informational
- **Bot replies** -- Automated responses

### Trend Tracking
Monitor how lead categories shift over time. If your positive reply rate is declining while negative responses are climbing, something changed -- your targeting, your copy, or your market. Category trends help you diagnose issues faster than raw reply rates ever could.

---

## Why This Is a Game-Changer

### Honest Metrics
Other tools inflate reply rates with bot responses and auto-replies. Outfound separates the signal from the noise. When you report a 5% positive human reply rate, that number means something real.

### Targeting Feedback
If you're getting a lot of "wrong person" or "not interested" replies, it's a targeting problem, not a copy problem. If you're getting "interested but not now," it's a timing issue. Lead categories give you diagnostic information, not just performance scores.

### Quality Over Quantity
A campaign with 100 replies where 60 are positive humans is dramatically better than a campaign with 200 replies where 150 are auto-replies. Lead category analytics makes this obvious at a glance.

### Campaign Comparison
Compare lead category distributions across campaigns to understand which audiences generate the highest-quality responses. Campaign A might have a lower total reply rate but a much higher positive-human ratio -- making it the better campaign by any meaningful measure.

# Campaign Analytics

### Performance Metrics Across Every Dimension

Campaign analytics in Outfound goes far beyond "how many emails were sent and how many replies came back." It ingests data from your sending platforms and tracks 50+ metrics across every campaign, every variant, every step, every client -- with time-series breakdowns, rolling windows, baseline comparisons, and direct attribution to revenue.

---

## What's Tracked

### Volume Metrics
- **Emails sent** -- Total and unique sends (distinguishes retries from new sends)
- **Replies received** -- Total reply count with breakdowns by type
- **Bounces** -- Hard and soft bounces tracked separately
- **Opens** -- Email open tracking (when enabled)
- **Clicks** -- Link click tracking (when enabled)

### Response Quality Metrics
- **Positive replies** -- Prospects who expressed interest
- **Negative replies** -- Declines and not-interested responses
- **Neutral replies** -- Ambiguous or informational responses
- **Human replies** -- Confirmed human-written responses
- **Bot replies** -- Auto-replies, out-of-office, and automated responses

### Timing Metrics
- **Average reply time** -- How quickly prospects respond after first contact
- **Average follow-up time** -- Time between a prospect reply and your team's follow-up

### Attribution Metrics (per campaign)
- **Sign-ups** -- New accounts attributed to the campaign
- **Meetings booked** -- Meetings attributed to the campaign
- **Paying customers** -- Revenue conversions attributed to the campaign
- **Website visits** -- Tracked visits attributed to the campaign

---

## How You Can Slice the Data

### By Time Period
View any metric as a time series -- daily, weekly, or monthly. Spot trends, identify seasonality, and understand how performance evolves over time.

### By Rolling Window
See performance across multiple rolling windows simultaneously: last 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 30 days, and all-time. One view shows you both the latest performance and the longer trend -- no toggling between date ranges.

### By Client
For agencies and multi-client operations, every metric is available per client. Compare client performance side by side, identify which clients need attention, and generate per-client reports.

### By Campaign
Drill into individual campaigns to see step-by-step performance. Which step gets the most replies? Which step has the highest bounce rate? Where do prospects drop off?

### By Variant
Every A/B variant is tracked independently with all the same metrics. See exactly which version of your email performs better -- not just on reply rates, but on positive reply rates, attribution, and revenue.

## Baseline Comparisons

Outfound automatically calculates baseline metrics for your account:

- **Median performance** across all campaigns
- **25th and 75th percentile** ranges
- **Standard deviation** from your norm

When you look at any campaign, you instantly see whether it's performing above or below your historical baseline. No more guessing whether a 4% reply rate is good or bad -- you see it in context.

**Why it's a game-changer:** Benchmarks from industry reports are meaningless because they don't account for your specific audience, offer, and sending infrastructure. Outfound benchmarks you against yourself, which is the only comparison that matters.

## Prospect Enrichment Overlay

Campaign analytics can be filtered and broken down by prospect enrichment data:

- **Industry** -- Which industries respond best?
- **Company size (headcount)** -- Do SMBs or enterprises engage more?
- **Seniority** -- Are VPs or Directors more responsive?
- **Department** -- Which departments convert?

**Why it matters:** Knowing that a campaign has a 5% reply rate is useful. Knowing that it has a 12% reply rate with VPs at mid-market SaaS companies is actionable. Enrichment overlays turn generic metrics into targeting insights.

## Client Summary Reports

For agencies managing multiple clients, Outfound generates comprehensive client summary reports with:

- Total sends, replies, and conversion metrics
- Performance trends over time
- Campaign-by-campaign breakdown
- Variant performance comparisons
- Attribution metrics (sign-ups, meetings, revenue)

Reports are exportable to CSV with full metric fidelity and custom date range selection.

## Real-Time Refresh

Campaign analytics are refreshed every 3 minutes. When your sending platform delivers a batch of emails, the results are reflected in Outfound's dashboard almost immediately. No waiting for nightly aggregation jobs or stale daily reports.

**Why it matters:** Outbound is iterative. You launch, you measure, you adjust. A 3-minute feedback loop means you can catch problems early and double down on winners fast.

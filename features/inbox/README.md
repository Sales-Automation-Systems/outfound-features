# Unified Inbox

### Every Outbound Conversation in One Place

Outfound's unified inbox pulls in every email conversation from all your sending platforms and clients into a single, searchable view. No more switching between Instantly, Smartlead, and EmailBison to find a conversation. No more digging through individual email accounts. Everything is here.

---

## What's in the Inbox

Every email conversation in your outbound operation:

- **Sent emails** -- Every outbound email delivered by your sending platforms
- **Received emails** -- Every reply from prospects
- **Failed emails** -- Emails that failed to deliver, with failure reasons
- **Archived conversations** -- Past conversations you've marked as handled
- **DNC-flagged emails** -- Conversations with contacts on your Do Not Contact list

Each conversation includes:
- Email subject and body content
- Sender and recipient details
- Timestamp
- Campaign association
- Lead category classification
- Conversation thread (full back-and-forth history)

---

## Filtering & Search

### By Status
Filter to see only sent emails, only received replies, only failures, or only archived conversations. Focus on what needs attention right now.

### By Campaign
See all conversations from a specific campaign. Useful when reviewing campaign performance or troubleshooting a specific outreach.

### By Lead Category
Filter by AI-classified lead categories -- show only positive replies, only meeting requests, or only out-of-office responses. Instantly prioritize high-value conversations.

### By Client
For agencies managing multiple clients, filter the inbox by client to see only their conversations.

### Conversation Threads
View the full back-and-forth thread of any conversation. See the original outbound email, the prospect's reply, any follow-ups, and the current status -- all in one view.

## Why It Matters

### Single Point of Access
Your team runs campaigns on Instantly, Smartlead, and EmailBison. Replies come back to different accounts on different platforms. Without a unified inbox, finding a specific conversation means logging into the right platform, finding the right account, and searching through that platform's inbox.

Outfound eliminates this entirely. Every conversation, regardless of platform or account, is in one place.

### Nothing Falls Through the Cracks
When replies are scattered across multiple platforms and email accounts, things get missed. A positive reply sits unnoticed in an account nobody checks frequently. An interested prospect goes cold because the notification was lost.

The unified inbox, combined with [Slack notifications](../integrations/slack.md) and [lead category filtering](../analytics/lead-category-analytics.md), ensures every reply is visible, classified, and actionable.

### Historical Reference
Need to check what was said to a prospect six months ago? It's in the inbox. Need to verify whether an email was actually sent? It's in the inbox. Need to audit what copy was used in a specific campaign? It's in the inbox.

The unified inbox is your complete historical record of every outbound conversation.
